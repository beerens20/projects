One Time Donation

We gladly accept donations through Paypal at any time to help support our cause.

These donations are used to assist area hospitals obtain necessary supplies such as memory boxes for families to keep mementos of their child, cameras for the hospitals to capture memories of their child, and casting supplies to memorialize their child's hands and feet.

We also use donations to put together folders that contain information with resources to assist families in their time of grief.
