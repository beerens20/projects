Our Board Members

Dana Wyant, President and Chairperson

Dana works with bereaved families as a Perinatal Bereavement Coordinator at a local hospital. Walk To Remember, Inc. has provided an opportunity to extend that outreach beyond the hospital and into our community. She is passionate about increasing awareness of the devastating affects of perinatal and infant loss as well as providing resources for area hospitals who care for these families. Dana and her husband Ken have resided in Huntertown for 24 years where they raised their three sons and are currently raising their very energetic doodles, Tucker and Finnley.

Shelley A. Horn, Board of Directors

Shelley became involved with Walk To Remember, Inc. in 2017 when she was asked to be a member of our Board of Directors. Her background as a Paralegal and experience establishing businesses has been an invaluable asset to help us obtain and manage our 501c3 status. Shelley and her husband Tim recently moved to Texas with their two dogs. She has recently obtained her license to drive their new Can-Am Spyder and is looking forward to exploring Texas on it.
