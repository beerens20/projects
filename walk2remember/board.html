Our Board Members

Dana Wyant, President

Dana works with bereaved families as a Perinatal Bereavement Coordinator at a local hospital. Walk To Remember, Inc. has provided an opportunity to extend that outreach beyond the hospital and into our community. She is passionate about increasing awareness of the devastating affects of perinatal and infant loss as well as providing resources for area hospitals who care for these families.

Dana and her husband Ken have resided in Huntertown for 24 years where they raised their three sons and are currently raising their very energetic doodles, Tucker and Finnley.

Shelley A. Horn

Shelley became involved with Walk To Remember, Inc. in 2017 when she was asked to be a member of our Board of Directors. Her background as a Paralegal and experience establishing businesses has been an invaluable asset to help us obtain and manage our 501c3 status.

Shelley and her husband Tim recently moved to Texas with their two dogs. She has recently obtained her license to drive their new Can-Am Spyder and is looking forward to exploring Texas on it.

Kelli Bunn, Vice President

Kelli became involved in Walk to Remember, Inc. when she and her husband, Zach, lost their daughter, Delaney on December 30, 2015. Kelli had a healthy, normal pregnancy, and was utterly blindsided when Delaney was stillborn at 39 weeks. Joining Walk to Remember, Inc. has allowed Kelli to focus on honoring her daughter, while helping others on their infant loss journey.

Kelli is a stay-at-home mom, photographer, and cake creator. She and Zach have 2 living children, Josephina and Ezekiel, who bring them so much joy.

Zach Bunn, Project Manager

Zach became involved in Walk to Remember, Inc. after losing daughter, Delaney Suzanne on December 30, 2015. Zach believes in assisting the Walk to Remember Committee and Board in providing a Walk in which grieving parents can find support and comfort from other grieving parents and support professionals.

Zach works for Professional Systems Installations as a Scheduling Coordinator/Manager. Zach is married to Kelli and they have 2 children, Josephina and Ezekiel, and Delaney in heaven.

Kyle Rebman

Kyle joined Walk to Remember, Inc in the Fall of 2017 as a board member after experiencing the stillbirth of his daughter, Hadleigh. Kyle wanted to be a part of an organization that helped give back to the families that have experienced infant and perinatal loss and give them the necessary resources they need during their time of unimaginable loss.

Kyle is currently a Director of Marketing and Admissions at a nursing facility in Auburn, IN. He, and his wife Ashley, reside in Fort Wayne with their two dogs, Dexter and Sammy, and are expecting their rainbow baby, Leighton Grace, in August 2018.

Cassie Goldsberry, Treasurer

Cassie is a nurse by trade and a momma of 4 with 1 of them receiving the ultimate privilege of living with our Heavenly Father. Cassie loves spending time with her husband, Zach, and she loves to read.

Kris Pfeiffer

Kris is a Registered Respiratory Therapist at a local hospital, where she works in all areas of the hospital. Her favorite areas are the birthplace and NICU. She is a single mother of two wonderful teenagers who keep her very busy and active. She truly enjoys donating her spare time to worthy causes which are close to her heart.

Sarah Ann Hidy, Secretary

Sarah Ann has been a nurse for 31 years, 29 of those years in Labor and Delivery. She became involved with Walk to Remember, Inc. because, as part of her job, she works with those experiencing perinatal and infant loss.

She is married to John Hidy, and they have 2 grown boys and 3 dogs.
